Physics

The Physics page is where applied mathematics really lives. Certain concepts learned in mathematics, such as calculus, were invented to be utilized in physics. The subject is often thought to be plug and chug maths, but that is far from the reality. Using this page, I will help the reader create intuition about these physical phenomena using quantitative methods and models.
